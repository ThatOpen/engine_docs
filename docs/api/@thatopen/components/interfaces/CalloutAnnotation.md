# CalloutAnnotation

The committed data for a single callout annotation.

## Properties

### center

> **center**: `Vector3`

Centre of the enclosure shape.

***

### elbow

> **elbow**: `Vector3`

Elbow — the bend between the extension line and its horizontal run.

***

### extensionEnd

> **extensionEnd**: `Vector3`

End of the horizontal extension line — anchor for the text label.

***

### halfH

> **halfH**: `number`

Half-height of the enclosure along the Z axis.

***

### halfW

> **halfW**: `number`

Half-width of the enclosure along the X axis.

***

### style

> **style**: `string`

Name of the [CalloutAnnotationStyle](CalloutAnnotationStyle.md) to use when rendering.

***

### text

> **text**: `string`

The annotation text.

***

### uuid

> **uuid**: `string`

Unique identifier.
